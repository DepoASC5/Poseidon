The #1 Cryptoportfolio Manager

About Us

Poseidon is a web based app that simplifies the awesome yet tedious process of Cryptocurrency portfolio management, by analyzing user inputted crypto amounts from a wide variety of coins and exchanges, we display portfolio data in a simple and easy to understand way.

Crafted With You In Mind

Poseidon was created by a group of software engineers with one vision in mind: Simplifying the management of their Crypto portfolios. The services that already existed failed to provide real time data on coin valuations and lacked a good User Interface, so they made one themselves. The idea started off simple but grows more useful with each update.
